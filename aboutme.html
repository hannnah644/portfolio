" About Me and Only Me "

I am Hannah Mitche Lomod, a simple daughter with big dreams. I know I’m not the smartest in the room, but I believe that when you have goals in life and you want to achieve them, you work hard to make them happen. With purpose in my heart, I’ll do my best, not just for me, but for my family who has forever been my inspiration.

My parents have sacrificed so much just to give me and my brother a good life. They worked tirelessly, putting our demands above their own, and that motivates me to keep going no matter how bad life gets. But I can’t do it without the help of our Almighty God. I want to achieve my goals, not only for myself but also to give back to them and show my gratitude for all they have done.

When I’m done with my schoolwork or feel overwhelmed by many tasks, I find joy in simple hobbies like watching anime and reading novels or comics. These stories transport me to different worlds, offering moments of escape and inspiration. They remind me that even in the face of challenges, strength and perseverance can lead to incredible outcomes.
